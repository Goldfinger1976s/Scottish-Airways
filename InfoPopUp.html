Flight Class Information

We offer 3 classes of travel:

First Class

When you travel in First you will experience personal service from the moment you check in until after you arrive. Enjoy unparalleled comfort and privacy from our ergonomically designed 2m (6 feet 6 inches) beds and a flexible dining experience that allows you to eat whenever it is convenient to you.

Business Class

Business class gives you options which allow you to manage your time as you choose. Enjoy the benefits of lounge access, enhanced comfort and facilities for working. With the world's first fully flat beds in business class now on even more of our longhaul services, you can truly relax and put your feet up.

Economy Class

Flying economy with us is all about great service at an affordable price. On all flights enjoy a professional service from our highly trained cabin crew. On our longhaul services enjoy complimentary drinks and meals. On our European and UK services we offer complimentary drinks and snacks.

Ticket Information

We offer 3 different ticket types:

Adult

An adult is anyone aged 18 or above. Every booking must consist of at least one adult. A booking can have a maximum of 9 adult tickets.

Child

A child is anyone between the ages of 5 and 17 inclusive. A child must be accompanyed by an adult. Also for every 3 children there must be one adult. Each booking can have a maximum of 8 child tickets.

Infant

An infant is a child aged 4 or below. Every infant must be accompanyed by a responsible adult. Each booking can have a maximum of 8 infant tickets.
