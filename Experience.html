The Experience

A Great Way to Fly

Step into a place where exclusivity is made more personal, where space can liberate your senses and the level of comfort will soothe your mind and body. Rediscover the romance of flying once again with Scottish Airways.

We've taken great care to perfect the details of your experience. With Scottish Airways, you will enjoy the highest standards of quality, service and innovation across all classes.

First Class

1st Class

Our First Class SkySuites on the aircraft has been designed with luxury, space and comfort in mind. It is your home away from home. Luxuriate in the state of the art seat in elegance that evokes the golden age of travel.

Upholstered in leather trimmed with Burrwood, the ScotSuite can recline to any angle, including a fully flat bed. As it reclines, the seat automatically adjusts to provide better support. Get the rest you deserve, and the comfort to which you are accustomed.

When you're ready to retire, simply inform your cabin crew, who will promptly turn your seat into a bed, complete with down-filled mattress, duvet, and a large pillow. Five-star, pampering service designed with you in mind.

Business Class

Business Class

Scottish Airways symbolises greater convenience, space, comfort and time-savings for the business traveller combining enhanced personal space, functionality and leading edge innovation.

Beyond a simple business class seat, the seat gives you unparalleled comfort, from multiple seating positions to a comfortable sleeping bed at an angle of 8 degrees. When the armrests are lowered, it also provides extra space to allow you more freedom to work, relax or sleep.
